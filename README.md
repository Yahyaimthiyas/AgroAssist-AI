# 🌾 AgroAssist AI

## AI-Powered Crop Recovery Prediction and Treatment Optimization Platform

---

## 📌 Project Overview

AgroAssist AI is an intelligent agricultural decision-support platform designed to help farmers make informed decisions after crop disease detection. Unlike traditional crop disease detection systems that only identify diseases from leaf images, AgroAssist AI goes beyond diagnosis by predicting crop recovery probability, estimating yield loss, and recommending optimized treatment plans.

The platform focuses on tomato disease management and aims to reduce crop losses through AI-driven insights, helping farmers take the right action at the right time.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Current crop disease detection systems can identify diseases from crop images but fail to answer critical questions that farmers face every day:

- Will the crop recover?
- How severe is the disease?
- How much yield loss can be expected?
- Which treatment should be applied?
- What is the most cost-effective solution?
- When should treatment be applied?
- Are there alternative treatment options?

Due to the lack of personalized recommendations and predictive analysis, farmers often make decisions based on assumptions, leading to reduced productivity and financial losses.

AgroAssist AI addresses this problem by providing complete AI-powered decision support rather than simple disease identification.

---

# 🚀 Proposed Solution

The farmer uploads:

- Crop Leaf Image
- Crop Age
- Farm Location
- Soil Information
- Treatment History (Optional)

The system then provides:

- Disease Detection
- Disease Severity Assessment
- Recovery Probability Prediction
- Yield Loss Estimation
- Treatment Recommendation
- Cost Analysis
- Action Plan

# 💡 Key Features

### 🔍 Disease Detection
Identify crop diseases from leaf images using Deep Learning.

### 📊 Severity Prediction
Classify disease progression into:

- Mild
- Moderate
- Severe

### 🌱 Recovery Prediction
Estimate the probability of crop recovery.

### 📉 Yield Loss Prediction
Predict expected crop production loss.

### 💊 Treatment Recommendation
Suggest the most effective treatment plan.

### 💰 Cost Optimization
Compare treatment effectiveness with estimated costs.

### 📄 PDF Report Generation
Generate downloadable farmer reports.

### 📚 Historical Analysis
Store previous diagnoses and recommendations.

# 🧠 Core Innovation

## CRTO Algorithm

### Crop Recovery and Treatment Optimization Algorithm

The CRTO Algorithm is the primary research contribution of AgroAssist AI.

It combines:

- Disease Type
- Disease Severity
- Crop Age
- Weather Conditions
- Soil Parameters
- Treatment History

to generate:

- Recovery Probability Score
- Yield Loss Estimate
- Treatment Ranking
- Cost Efficiency Score
- Recommended Action Plan
